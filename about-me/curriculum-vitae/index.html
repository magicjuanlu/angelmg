The Brain Computer Interface (BCI) is a technology that processes brain activity measured by the sensor and operates the computer and machine. The information and communication system that automatically controls things over the Internet by providing communication functions to objects and sensors around us is called the Internet of Things (IOT). In the laboratory, we are combining BCI and IOT technologies to create systems based on a future-oriented mind that makes people's lives easier.
